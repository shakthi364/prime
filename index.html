About Our Company

We'll Help You Grow

Prime Agri Business & Plantation (Pvt) Ltd is a Sri Lankan agricultural company focused on sustainable pineapple cultivation and plantation-based investment opportunities. We combine modern farming methods with responsible land management to deliver premium quality produce while creating long-term value for investors, farmers, and communities.

  • Sustainable Practices: Environmentally responsible farming
  • Premium Quality: Cultivating high-quality pineapples
  • Long-Term Value: Creating sustainable returns for investors
  • Community Focus: Empowering local communities

OUR MISSION & VISION

At Prime Agri Business & Plantation, we are committed to sustainable agriculture, responsible land management, and creating long-term value for our investors, communities, and future generations.

Our Mission

To cultivate premium quality pineapples through sustainable and innovative farming practices while delivering exceptional value to our investors, empowering local communities, and contributing to a greener future.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted and sustainable pineapple agribusiness, recognized for excellence in cultivation, responsible stewardship, and creating enduring prosperity for generations to come.
